Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on PráticOS?

Download the app from App Store or Google Play, open the app and choose one of the login options: Google, Apple or Email.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly in the app: go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and removes all your data.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use Firebase for storage, with encryption in transit and at rest. We follow security best practices.

How do I contact support?

Send an email to praticos@rafsoft.com.br. We respond within 24 business hours.
