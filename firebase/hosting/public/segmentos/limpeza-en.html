FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Get your questions answered before you start

Do I need to pay to use it?

No! The free plan is real and has no time limit. You can use it forever with up to 30 photos/month. Our pricing is transparent and you don't need to talk to a salesperson.

How does geolocation check-in work?

The employee opens the app when arriving at the service location. The system automatically records the location, ensuring they are actually at the client's address.

Can I create different checklists for each type of cleaning?

Yes! You can create specific checklists for residential, commercial, post-construction cleaning, etc. Each with tasks appropriate for that type of service.

Will my employees be able to use it?

Absolutely! PraticOS was made to be as simple as using WhatsApp. If you know how to use a phone, you know how to use PraticOS.

Can the client see the report?

Yes! You can send an automatic report with all photos and the filled checklist. The client receives it by email or WhatsApp.
