Financial System Overview

PráticOS offers a complete and integrated financial system for payment control, discounts and billing tracking. All transactions are recorded with detailed history and linked to Work Orders.

RBAC Integration

Access to financial data is controlled by role. Technicians and Supervisors cannot view values, ensuring information security.

Partial Payments

Record multiple payments with complete transaction history

Discounts

Apply discounts with description and full traceability

Dashboard

Track billing and receivables in real-time

Payment Status

The system uses three statuses to indicate the financial situation of each Work Order:

Pending

No payment recorded. The total WO value is pending.

Partial

Partial payment recorded. There is still a balance to receive from the customer.

Paid

Fully paid. No pending balance for this WO.

Technical Note

The "Partial" status is automatically calculated based on the paid amount. In the database, only "Paid" and "Pending" are stored to maintain compatibility with existing filters.

Payment Management

The payments screen centralizes all financial operations of a WO, allowing you to record payments, apply discounts and view the complete history.

How to Access

  1. Open an existing Work Order
  2. In the Summary section, click on Total or the Payment status
  3. The payment management screen will open automatically

Available Features

Financial Summary

  • WO Total
  • Applied discounts
  • Amount already paid
  • Remaining balance

Record Transaction

  • Toggle between Payment and Discount
  • Value pre-filled with balance
  • Optional note field
  • Quick "Pay total" action

History

  • List of payments and discounts
  • Sorted by date
  • Swipe to remove
  • Description and responsible

Business Rules

The system applies validations to ensure financial data integrity and correct payment flow.

Payments by WO Status

The ability to record payments depends on the current Work Order status:

WO Status Can Record Payment? Auto-Approval
Quote ✓ Yes ✓ Transitions to Approved
Approved ✓ Yes ✗ No
In Progress ✓ Yes ✗ No
Completed ✓ Yes ✗ No
Cancelled ✗ No ✗ No

Quote Auto-Approval

When recording a payment or discount on a WO with Quote status, it is automatically approved and transitions to Approved status. This eliminates the need for manual approval before recording the first payment.

Applied Validations

  • Minimum value: Payment or discount value must be greater than zero
  • Maximum value: Value cannot exceed the WO remaining balance
  • Valid status: WO cannot be cancelled (quotes are allowed and auto-approved)

Financial Dashboard

The dashboard provides a consolidated view of company billing, with real-time indicators and customer ranking.

How to Access

Access through Side menu → Dashboard. Requires viewFinancialReports permission (available for Admin and Manager).

Available Indicators

Total Billing

Sum of total values of all WOs in the selected period

Amount Received

Sum of all recorded payments, including partial ones

Receivables

Pending balance from WOs not fully paid

Paid WOs

Number of WOs with complete payment

Customer Ranking

The dashboard displays customers sorted by value, allowing identification of:

  • Top customers: Sorted by total WO value
  • Top debtors: Sorted by pending receivable amount

Print (PDF)

The Work Order PDF includes the complete financial summary, automatically adapting to the payment status.

PDF Information

Totals Summary

  • Services (when applicable)
  • Products (when applicable)
  • Subtotal
  • Discount (when applied)
  • Total
  • Already paid (in partial payments)

Summary Footer

The footer displays the financial status with differentiated colors:

Situation Displayed Text Color
Fully paid TOTAL PAID Green
Partial payment REMAINING BALANCE Orange
No payment TOTAL DUE Blue

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I record payment on a quote?

No. Payments are only enabled from "Approved" status. First approve the quote, then record the payment.

How do I apply a discount?

On the payments screen, switch to "Discount" in the top selector, enter the amount and add a description (e.g., "Loyalty discount").

Can I remove a recorded payment?

Yes. In the transaction history, swipe the item left and confirm removal. The action is recorded in the history.

What does "Partial" status mean?

It indicates that payments have been recorded, but the total amount has not yet been settled. The remaining balance is shown in the summary.

How do I mark a WO as fully paid?

Use the "Pay total" quick action on the payments screen, or record a payment with the exact remaining balance amount.

Do partial payments appear in the PDF?

Yes. The PDF shows "Already paid" with the received amount and "REMAINING BALANCE" highlighted in orange in the footer.

Can technicians see financial values?

No. Technicians and Supervisors don't have access to financial data. Only Admin, Manager and Consultant (own WOs) can view values.

Does the dashboard consider partial payments?

Yes. The "Amount Received" indicator sums all recorded payments, and "Receivables" shows only the pending balance.
