Procedures Overview

Procedures are dynamic forms that allow you to standardize and document each step of your service. They can be safety checklists, authorization terms, technical inspections, satisfaction surveys and much more.

Benefits of Procedures

Procedures help reduce errors, ensure quality, create photographic evidence of services and standardize service delivery across your entire team.

Checklists

Ensure all important steps are verified

Terms & Authorizations

Document customer acceptances and authorizations

Photos per Item

Capture photographic evidence linked to each verification

Types of Procedures

PráticOS offers two types of procedures to meet your company's needs:

My Procedures

Procedures created by your company. Fully customizable and exclusive to your business. You can create, edit and delete as needed.

Global Procedures

Ready-made templates based on industry standards for each segment. Can be imported and customized for your company. Includes safety checklists, authorization terms and more.

Global Procedures by Segment

Global templates are specific to each segment (automotive, HVAC, smartphones, etc.) and follow industry best practices to increase quality and reduce returns.

Creating Procedures

Create custom procedures to standardize your company's processes.

How to Access

  1. Go to Settings in the bottom menu
  2. Tap Procedures in the Management section
  3. Tap the + button in the top right corner

Configuring the Procedure

Basic Information

  • Title: Procedure name (e.g., "Delivery Checklist")
  • Description: Explanation of when to use (optional)
  • Active: Defines if it appears for selection in work orders

Procedure Items

  • Label: Question or verification text
  • Type: Response format (text, yes/no, etc.)
  • Required: If it must be filled
  • Allow Photos: If photos can be attached to the item

Managing Procedures

  • Edit: Swipe the procedure right or tap on it
  • Delete: Swipe left and confirm deletion
  • Deactivate: Edit and uncheck the "Active" option to temporarily hide

Field Types

Each procedure item can have different response types, adapting to the needs of each verification:

Type Description Usage Example
Text Free text field for descriptive answers Notes, defect description
Number Numeric field for measurements and quantities Temperature, voltage, mileage
Yes/No Toggle for simple confirmations Equipment working? Customer present?
Selection List of predefined options Condition: Good/Fair/Poor
Checklist Multiple selectable options Items checked, parts replaced
Photo Only Item that requires only photographic record Equipment photo, license plate photo

Importing Global Procedures

PráticOS offers ready-made templates based on industry standards that you can import and customize.

How to Import

  1. Go to Settings → Procedures
  2. Scroll to the Global Procedures section
  3. View templates available for your segment
  4. Tap Import on the desired template
  5. Confirm the import

Customize After Import

After importing, the procedure becomes "yours" and can be freely edited. Add, remove or modify items according to your company's needs.

Global Template Examples

Using Procedures in Work Orders

Procedures are attached to Work Orders to document each step of the service.

Attaching a Procedure

  1. Open a Work Order
  2. In the Forms section, tap Add
  3. Select the desired procedure from the list
  4. The procedure will be attached with "Pending" status

Procedure Status

Pending

Procedure attached but without any filling.

In Progress

Filling started but still incomplete.

Completed

All required items have been filled.

Filling the Procedure

  1. Tap on the procedure in the work order list
  2. Fill each item according to its type
  3. Add photos when needed (camera or gallery)
  4. Status updates automatically upon completion

Photos & Evidence

Capture photos directly in procedure items to create complete service evidence.

Photo Capture

  • Take photos directly from the app
  • Select from device gallery
  • Multiple photos per item
  • Preview before saving

Storage

  • Automatic cloud upload
  • Linked to the work order and specific item
  • Organized by procedure
  • Accessible from any device

Items with Required Photo

If an item is marked as "required" and "allows photos", at least one photo must be added for the procedure to be considered complete.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the same procedure in multiple work orders?

Yes! The procedure is a template. Each time you attach it to a work order, a new instance is created for independent filling.

What happens if I edit a procedure?

Edits only affect new work orders. Already attached procedures keep the original version (snapshot) to preserve history.

Can I remove a procedure from a work order?

Yes, swipe the procedure left in the work order forms list and confirm removal.

Are global procedures automatically updated?

No. When you import, you create a copy that becomes independent. Future updates to globals don't affect your version.

Can I have inactive procedures?

Yes. Inactive procedures don't appear for selection in new work orders, but remain accessible in work orders where they were already attached.

Is there a limit of items per procedure?

There's no technical limit, but we recommend keeping procedures objective and focused to facilitate field filling.

Do procedure photos appear in the work order gallery?

Photos are linked to the specific procedure item, organized separately from the work order's general photos.

Can I create procedures specific to service types?

Yes! Create specific procedures and manually attach them to work orders according to service type. Automatic linking by service is under development.
