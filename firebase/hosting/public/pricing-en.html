Questions

Pricing FAQ

How does upgrading work?

You can upgrade at any time through the app. The value is adjusted proportionally to the remaining period of your current plan.

Can I downgrade?

Yes! You can change to a smaller plan at any time. The change takes effect in the next billing cycle.

How is billing done?

Billing is monthly, via App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). You can cancel anytime, without penalties.

Do I need a card to start?

No! The Free plan is completely free and doesn't require credit card registration. Only register when you want to upgrade.

What happens if I exceed the limits?

You'll receive a warning before reaching the limit. If you exceed it, new photos/checklists are pending until upgrade or next month.

What's the difference between form and checklist?

They're the same thing! Checklists (or forms) are customizable templates for inspections, reports and service tracking.
