Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I create an account on PraticOS?

Just download the app, open it, and follow the registration instructions on the home screen.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

On the login screen, click "Forgot my password" and follow the instructions to reset it.

How do I contact support?

You can send an email to praticos@rafsoft.com.br or use the form on the home page of this site.

Is my data safe on PraticOS?

Yes, we follow best security and privacy practices to protect your information.
