What's new in this version:

• User Profiles System: Complete permission control by role (Owner, Administrator, Manager, Supervisor, Technician, and Viewer). Each role has specific access to app features.

• Partial Payments: Record and track multiple payments for a single work order, with full transaction history in the PDF.

• Improved Search: New search with infinite scroll, chip filters, and due date indicator with visual highlight for overdue orders.

• Dynamic Forms: Create custom checklists and procedures, with multiple photo selection and swipe navigation in the gallery.

• Profile Editing: Update your profile data and photo directly in the app.

• Invitation System: Invite collaborators by email to your company.

• Interface Improvements: Work order list redesign following Apple HIG, fully functional dark mode, and several visual fixes.

• Enhanced PDF: Optimized layout with store links and highlights for paid amounts.
